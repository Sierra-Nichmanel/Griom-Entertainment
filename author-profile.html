Evelyn Thorne

Historical Fiction Literary Noir Screenwriting

"A master of atmosphere and human frailty, Thorne crafts narratives that linger long after the final page is turned." — The Paris Review

Evelyn Thorne is an award-winning novelist and screenwriter based in Edinburgh. Her work often explores the intersection of memory, architecture, and forgotten histories. Since joining Griom Literary Entertainment in 2018, her debut novel, *The Gilded Archive*, has been translated into twenty-four languages and is currently being adapted for television by HBO.
