The Vision for Nigerian Media.

Our History

Founded on the principle that African literature deserves a seat at the center of the global entertainment stage, Griom began as a boutique literary agency in Lagos. We quickly realized that the gap between a written story and a cinematic production was wide, yet full of potential. Over the years, we have evolved into a full-service creative agency that bridges this gap, acting as the connective tissue between brilliant writers and visionary directors.

The Global Creative Pulse

Our vision extends beyond local boundaries. We see a future where Nigerian creative media is not just a 'niche' interest, but a cornerstone of global pop culture. By professionalizing the talent pipeline and ensuring our creators have world-class legal and creative representation, we are building the infrastructure for a sustainable, global-facing creative economy.
