Submission Guidelines

visibility What we look for

  • • Compelling voices in Fiction, Non-Fiction, and Young Adult literature.
  • • Diverse stories that explore unique cultural perspectives.
  • • Well-researched non-fiction that offers new insights.

edit_note Formatting requirements

  • • Standard manuscript format (Times New Roman, 12pt, double-spaced).
  • • Files must be in PDF or .docx format.
  • • Include a one-page synopsis and the first three chapters.

send_and_archive How to submit

  • • Use the "Manuscript Submission" tab on the form below.
  • • Include your full legal name and contact details.
  • • Wait 4-6 weeks for our editorial team to review your material.
